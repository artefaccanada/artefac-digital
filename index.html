About artefac

Two decades of Canadian business.
One sharp AI focus.

artefac was founded in Mississauga, Ontario in 2004. What started as a digital solutions company has grown into a multi-venture North American operation — exporting products and services to customers across the United States through artefac.com since day one in 2004.

For over 20 years, artefac.com has served US-based buyers — from retail furniture to digital products — shipping and delivering across all 50 states. That cross-border track record means we understand both Canadian and American business environments inside out.

artefac.digital is our dedicated AI automation division — bringing two decades of Canadian and US business operations into the AI era. We are one of the very few Canadian AI firms with a proven 20+ year cross-border operating history.
